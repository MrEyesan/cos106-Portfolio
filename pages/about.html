About Me

IT professional, aspiring cybersecurity engineer, web agency founder, and lifelong learner based in Lagos, Nigeria.

Biography

Oritsejemineyin Eyesan Sunday

I'm a Lagos-based IT professional transitioning from a Senior Graphic Designer role into cybersecurity and cloud infrastructure. My journey started at Aptech Computer College, where I spent two years deepening my knowledge of networking, ethical hacking, and cloud computing — earning the Aptech Certified Network Engineer qualification.

I went on to complete a structured cybersecurity practicum at Bincom ICT Institute, working through real-world scenarios: Cowrie honeypot deployment, DVWA exploitation, SQL injection, ModSecurity WAF configuration, AWS IAM privilege escalation simulation, ELK Stack SIEM deployment, and a full enterprise pentest simulation.

Alongside my technical work, I founded Narhbiss Web Agency, where I lead operations, UI/UX, client acquisition, and project management. Our first paying client was MONSUF, a Lagos-based medical nonprofit.

I'm currently pursuing a BSc in Computer Science at MIVA Open University and actively hunting for IT support, helpdesk, and cybersecurity roles.
